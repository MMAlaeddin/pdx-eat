Best PDX Eats

Mariam & Daisy are here to share the best places to fill your belly in Portland, OR. The restaurants are categorized by cuisine. We will also include price points and recommended dishes.

Asian

Thai Peacock - $$

  • Thai Peacock is a Thai restaurant in Downtown Portland offerings the main thai dishes we love to eat. As a sister restaurant of Mee Sen Thai Eatery in N Portland, Thai Peacock offers some of the same dishes, but also other dishes.
    • Recommended dish: Pad Rua Mitt

Pho Oregon - $

  • Pho Oregon is your no frills, best pho you've ever had restaurant. If you want authentic Vietnamese food with minimal service, you'll find what you need here.
    • Recommended dish: Pho Tai

Italian

Il Solito - $$$$

  • Il Solito is a modern Italian restaurant that crafts cocktails and serves in a beautifully built Vintage Kimpton. Overall, you have a very attentive staff in a pristine atmosphere.
    • Recommended dish: Squash Ravioli

Oven & Shaker Pizza - $$$$

  • Oven & Shaker is a lively pizzeria in the heart of the Pearl District. Wood-fired pies and top shelf cocktails and curated for total enjoyment and lounging.
    • Recommended dish: Margherita Pizza

Mexican

Guero - $$$

  • Portland's take on the Mexican torta does not have competition. As a sandwich of its own kind, Guero takes the traditional torta and pairs it with top of the line mezcals. Enjoy a torta or two - that's their main sh'bang.
    • Recommended dish: Torta de Hamon

La Quebrada Taqueria - $

  • La Quebrada is going to knock your socks off! This food truck serves some of the tastiest and spiciest mexican food in Portland! A staff that gives you extra of everything for free and a cart that has a patio.
    • Recommended dish: PDX Hot Burrito

Vegan / Vegetarian

Blossoming Lotus - $$$

  • At Blossoming Lotus you'll feel as if you just walked into a zen zone. With the meditative atmosphere and raw vegan menu, you'll feel a sense of rejuvenation when you walk out those doors.
    • Recommended dish: Chana Curry

Prasad - $$$

  • Prasad is a cafe located in a yoga studio (Yoga Pearl) that caters to those who are nutritiously conscious. From fresh pressed juices to quinoa bowls, Prasad bridges the gap between good food and cautious consumption.
    • Recommended dish: Chipotle Chili Quinoa Bowl
